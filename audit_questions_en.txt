AUDIT QUESTIONS - ENGLISH

--- MARKETING ---
1. Is your listing published on at least one platform (e.g., Airbnb, Booking)?
   (Sub: URL of your main listing)
2. Are your property photos bright, well-framed, and professionally taken (or professional quality)?
3. Is your listing title catchy and highlighting your main asset (e.g., "Sea View", "City Center")?
   (Sub: What is your current listing title?)
4. Is your text description complete, engaging, and telling a "story" that makes people want to book?
5. Have you clearly identified your "target clientele" (e.g., families, couples, digital nomads) for this property?
   (Sub: Describe your target clientele)
6. Do you actively request reviews and maintain a rating above 4.8 stars out of 5?
   (Sub: What is your current average rating?)
7. Do you use social media (e.g., Instagram, Pinterest) to specifically promote this property?
   (Sub: Link to your page (e.g., Instagram))
8. Do you offer a 3D "virtual tour" or professional video of your property?
   (Sub: Link to virtual tour or video)
9. Do you have a personal website to generate direct bookings (off-platform) for this property?
   (Sub: URL of your direct booking site)
10. Do you (legally) collect guest emails for retention actions (e.g., -10% promo for future stay)?

--- EXPERIENCE ---
1. Is the property impeccably clean (hotel quality) before each arrival?
2. Is the check-in process simple, clear, and 100% reliable (e.g., functional key box, smart lock)?
   (Sub: What is your check-in method?)
3. Do you provide a welcome booklet (digital or physical) with at least vital information (Wi-Fi, rules, contacts)?
   (Sub: Link to your digital welcome booklet (if applicable))
4. Do you respond to guest messages in less than one hour (during daytime hours)?
5. Is your welcome booklet complete (appliance guides, emergencies, local recommendations) beyond the basics?
6. Do you provide a thoughtful "welcome basket" (e.g., coffee, tea, snacks, water or wine bottle)?
   (Sub: What does your welcome basket contain?)
7. Do you use automated messages (confirmation, D-1, mid-stay, departure) for smooth communication?
   (Sub: What automated messaging tool do you use?)
8. Do you personalize the welcome (e.g., personalized welcome note, attention for a birthday)?
9. Have you set up partnerships (home chef, VTC, babysitting) that you actively offer to guests?
   (Sub: Example of partner)
10. Do you anticipate "unspoken" needs (e.g., umbrella available, universal chargers, board games)?

--- OPERATIONS ---
1. Do you have a cleaning solution (yourself or a team) that is 100% reliable?
   (Sub: Name of your cleaning provider (if external))
2. Do you have at least two (ideally three) complete sets of bed linen and towels per bed/person?
   (Sub: How many complete linen sets do you have?)
3. Do you have a detailed cleaning checklist (written or visual) that your team (or you) follows to the letter?
4. Do you actively manage the stock of consumables (soap, toilet paper, coffee, sponges...) to never run out?
5. Do you have a maintenance provider (plumber, electrician) reachable in case of emergency on weekends?
   (Sub: Name of your maintenance provider)
6. Do you have a professional solution for laundry (external service or efficient on-site washer/dryer)?
7. Do you use software (Channel Manager or PMS) to synchronize your calendars if you are on multiple platforms?
   (Sub: What software do you use?)
8. Do you have a "quality control" system (e.g., cleaning team sends you photos) after each cleaning?
9. Do you have written procedures (SOPs) for common tasks (check-in, check-out, handling a breakdown)?
10. Do you estimate that your management (cleaning, communication, maintenance) is automated or delegated to more than 80%?

--- PRICING ---
1. Have you defined a "floor" base price for your nightly rate (based on your costs)?
   (Sub: What is your floor price per night (€)?)
2. Have you defined separate cleaning fees that exactly cover the actual cost of cleaning?
   (Sub: What is the amount of your cleaning fees (€)?)
3. Do you actively differentiate your prices between high season and low season?
4. Do you have different prices for weekdays (Mon-Thu) and weekends (Fri-Sun)?
5. Do you analyze (at least 1x/month) the prices of your 5 direct competitors?
6. Do you manually adjust your prices upward for local events (concerts, fairs, school holidays)?
7. Have you defined dynamic minimum stay rules (e.g., 2 nights on weekends, 7 nights in summer) to optimize your calendar?
8. Do you use an external dynamic pricing tool (e.g., PriceLabs, Wheelhouse) to automate your prices?
   (Sub: What pricing tool do you use?)
9. Do you offer tiered rates (e.g., -10% week, -25% month) to attract long stays?
10. Do you know and track your "RevPAR" (Revenue Per Available Room)?
    (Sub: What is your average RevPAR (€)?)

--- ACCOMMODATION ---
1. Is the property 100% compliant with safety standards (smoke detectors, CO, fire extinguisher)?
2. Is the Wi-Fi fast (fiber/high-speed), reliable, and covering 100% of the property (no "dead zone")?
   (Sub: What is the download speed (in Mbit/s)?)
3. Is the kitchen equipped with all essential and quality utensils for a guest to really cook?
4. Is the bedding (mattress, pillows, duvet) hotel quality and comfortable (not "budget")?
5. Is the decoration neat, modern (or themed), and "depersonalized" (no family photos)?
6. Do you provide "experiential" equipment (e.g., Netflix, Bluetooth speaker, Nespresso machine with capsules)?
   (Sub: Example of experiential equipment provided)
7. Do you have a dedicated workspace (real desk, good chair, good lighting) for remote workers?
8. Is your property optimized for your target clientele (e.g., baby cot and high chair if targeting families)?
9. Have you invested in a "Wow" or "Instagrammable" asset (e.g., Jacuzzi, exceptional view, swing, green wall)?
   (Sub: Describe your "Wow" asset)
10. Do you plan an annual "refresh" budget (painting, replacing worn linen, cushions)?

--- LEGAL & FINANCE ---
1. Have you verified that your condominium regulations (if applicable) explicitly allow short-term rentals?
2. Have you declared your activity to the town hall and obtained a registration number (if required in your city)?
   (Sub: What is your registration number?)
3. Do you collect and remit the tourist tax (if not automatically managed by the platform)?
4. Do you declare 100% of your rental income to taxes?
5. Have you taken out specific insurance (Professional Liability / PNO) in addition to Airbnb/Booking guarantee?
   (Sub: Name of your insurance company)
6. Do you have clear internal rules that are displayed and that guests legally accept when booking?
7. Have you chosen an optimized tax status (e.g., LMNP with actual expenses) for your activity?
   (Sub: What is your tax status?)
8. Do you keep analytical accounting (a "P&L") that precisely tracks all your costs (cleaning, products, electricity, commissions...)?
9. Do you work with an accountant specialized in real estate or furnished rentals?
   (Sub: Name of the accountant or firm)
10. Do you calculate (at least 1x/quarter) your net profitability (Net Profit) and your ROI (Return on Investment)?
